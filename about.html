The University of British Columbia and Simon Fraser University Sikh Students' Associations cordially invite the BC Sikh community to Stand Up and Take Action.

What is Nihaal?

  • Nihaal can be defined as to be englightened or blessed.
  • Nihaal is a joint effort by UBC and SFU Sikh Students' Associations to bring a new and unique approach to religious education and awareness.
  • The family-oriented show focuses on a different theme each year and showcases it with the help of Sikhism-based skits, movies, inspirational speakers, gatka performances, prizes and comedy.
