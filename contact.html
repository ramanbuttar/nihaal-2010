The University of British Columbia and Simon Fraser University Sikh Students' Associations cordially invite the BC Sikh community to Stand Up and Take Action.

Sikh Students' Association of University of British Columbia

The University of British Columbia's Sikh Students' Association was the first establishment of its kind in all of North America. Since its inception in 1984, the UBC SSA has grown to be a significant part of the Sikh youth in British Columbia.

This year, SSA UBC is celebrating 25 years of its establishment as a valued organization in the community.

For more information, visit SSA UBC.

Simon Fraser University Sikh Students' Association

Simon Fraser University's Sikh Students' Association was founded in the late 1990s to create an avenue for Sikh students to share and promote their values. This club provides an opportunity to all Sikh students to learn more about their religion and educate others though various community services.

This year, SFU SSA is celebrating 10 years of its establishment as a valued organization in the community.

For more information, visit SFU SSA.
