Parking

  • To gain access to the Massey Theatre parking lot, enter the driveway between the theatre and Moody Park Arena on 8th Avenue, just east of 8th Avenue and 8th Street intersection.
  • Additional free parking is available on a first come, first serve basis in the New Westminster Secondary School parking lot, which is adjacent to Massey Theatre.
  • Free street parking is also available.
